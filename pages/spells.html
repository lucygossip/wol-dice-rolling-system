Spell Information

Spell Information

  • Spell damage is determined by the rank of the spell, not the rank of the user
  • The user's rank determines the chance of success of a spell depending on the spell's rank, e.g. a wanderer has a low chance of succeeding with a legendary spell
  • The outcome is determined by rolling a 1d8. A roll of 1 is always a miss, the rest of the rolls are determined by the chance of success, e.g. A 7/8 chance of success means rolls 2-8 will succeed
