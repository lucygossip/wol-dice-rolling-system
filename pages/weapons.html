World of Legends - Dice Rolling System

General Information

Notes

  • The base damage is the lowest amount of damage a weapon or spell can do. The base increases by 1 per every increase in your rank. The base damage listed in this tool is the amount for a user at Civilian rank.

Weapon Information

Notes

  • The actual amount of damage a weapon will do depends on the dice roll. A higher roll will give a higher amount of damage. The ranges are: 1 = Miss | 2 = Base | 3 = Base + 1 | 4 = Base + 2 | 5 = Base + 3 | 6 = Base + 4
  • The Last Barrage and Hot Shot deal a static amount of damage and therefore do not follow the above system.
  • All staffs have a base damage of 0, therefore all dice rolls will output 0. Staffs instead add a buff to spells. The amount of buff will be listed under the "Buff" stat. Please use the Spell tool to find out more about spell damage.

Spell Information

Notes

  • You are allowed to do any spell from this list. Please be aware that books in the Hall of Attainment may mention spells that are not on it. If this is the case, they cannot be used in a duel.
  • Magic becomes available at Wanderer rank. Your character is only able to perform spells listed at their rank or higher. If you attempt a spell above your rank, it will automatically fail.
